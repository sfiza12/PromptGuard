What PromptGuard Actually Does

Gemini Classification

Prompts are wrapped as untrusted data and classified by Gemini into ALLOW, WARN, or BLOCK with risk score, threat level, attack type, and reasoning.

Fail-Closed Fallback

If Gemini is unavailable, local scanning still catches obvious attacks and the firewall holds uncertain safe-looking prompts instead of forwarding blindly.

Live Routing And Audit

Safe prompts can be forwarded to Ollama, OpenAI, Claude, or Gemini. Every decision is stored in the audit dashboard with forwarding and warning history.

2. DecideALLOW continues, WARN pauses for review, BLOCK stops.
